E-Bike Design Challenge

About Sonae e-bike Design Challenge

About the Challenge

This challenge will test skills and thinking powers of students with regards to a workable E-Bike design. We expect the students to be high on research on the latest in E-Bikes in the world and then put their knowledge, skills and innovation powers to create an E-Bike design that fits best for Indian roads which have international standard


Vision

To help eligible students create an E-Bike from scratch and make it commercially viable for the market. This will be a live experience for the students that will make them industry ready and productive in their professional endeavors. The winning teams can be offered permanent employment at Research team of Sonae EV Pvt. Ltd.
